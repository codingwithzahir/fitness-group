COMPARE MEMBERSHIP



Platinum Membership

  • Access: All gym locations and facilities.
  • Benefits:
    • Unlimited access to classes (e.g., yoga, spin, HIIT).
    • Complimentary personal training sessions.
    • Priority booking for classes and events.
    • Access to spa, nutritional consultations, and wellness workshops.
  • Cost: Highest monthly fee.

Gold Membership

  • Access: Most gym locations and facilities.
  • Benefits:
    • Access to standard classes.
    • Discounted personal training sessions.
    • Some wellness workshops included.
    • Limited guest passes each month.
  • Cost: Moderate monthly fee.

Silver Membership

  • Access: Limited access to select gym locations.
  • Benefits:
    • Basic gym access with limited classes.
    • No personal training sessions included.
    • Fewer guest passes available.
  • Cost: Lowest monthly fee.
