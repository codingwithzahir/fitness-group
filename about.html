Missions of the GYM

The principles provide a framework to help you maximise the effectiveness of your workout and ensure you are getting the most out of it. The 10 principles of fitness training are Specificity, Overload, Progression, Individualisation, Recovery, Variation, Reversibility, Balance, Warm-up and Cool Down, and Cardiovascular/Respiratory Endurance.

Core values of a gym:

  • Influence daily operations and long-term growth.
  • Shape how members perceive the brand.
  • Guide staff interactions with clients.
  • Set the gym apart from competitors.
  • Represent the gym's culture and expectations.
